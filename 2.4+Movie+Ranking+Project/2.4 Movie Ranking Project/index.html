The 3 best movies according to Steve

My top 3 movies of all time

Terminator 2

This movie is a classic. Movie about a liquid metal man from the future that tries to kill main character John Conner so that he doesn't grow up to become the leader that takes out the machines as they try to kill all mankind. Arnold Schwarzenneger is also a machine that John sent from the future to go back and help protect him against the T1000 liquid metal man.

Alien

Horror movie with Sigorny Weaver. Lots of blood and gore. Sigorny (Ridley) and her team of scientists set out to scout the universe for life, and they do indeed find life but not the type that they wanted to!

Top Gun

American classic. Young Tom Cruise is a fighter pilot eager to take on the enemy forces from around the world. He meets a woman and falls in love with her! lots of action and romance!
